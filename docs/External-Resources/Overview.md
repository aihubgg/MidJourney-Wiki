### Image Scaling Tools 

These tools can all help you up-scale images (better than just basic
upscaling). Also see our guide at [Scaling Images
Up](MidJourney:_Scaling_Images_Up "wikilink") for comparisons.

  Link Name                     URL                                                       Notes
  ----------------------------- --------------------------------------------------------- ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Gigapixel                     <https://www.topazlabs.com/gigapixel-ai>                  Paid upscaler tool (also arguably the best from the comparison at [Scaling Images Up](MidJourney:_Scaling_Images_Up "wikilink")). Offers a free trial.
  Deep AI\'s Super Resolution   <https://deepai.org/machine-learning-model/torch-srgan>   Free web-based upscaler
  Photoshop                     <https://www.adobe.com/products/photoshop.html>           Does a lot more than upscale, but does have a preserve detail upscale that\'s better than a basic upscale (again see comparison at [Scaling Images Up](MidJourney:_Scaling_Images_Up "wikilink"))
